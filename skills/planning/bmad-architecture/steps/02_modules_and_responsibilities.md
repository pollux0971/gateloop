# Step 2 — Modules and responsibilities (with FR coverage)

Decompose the system into modules. For EACH module, write:

- its single responsibility, and
- the PRD Functional Requirements it covers, by id: `covers FR-1, FR-2`.

Every FR-n from the PRD must be covered by at least one module — this is the
FR→module coverage rule (enforced structurally here, and exhaustively by the
backend dry-run). Finish by listing the Risks.
