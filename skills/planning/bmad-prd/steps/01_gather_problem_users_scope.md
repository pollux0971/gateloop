# Step 1 — Gather problem, users, and scope (halts for input)

Ask the operator and record their answers. **Halt here for input** — do not
invent requirements.

1. **Problem** — what problem does this product solve, and why now?
2. **Users** — who are the primary users? (name them explicitly)
3. **Scope** — what is *in scope* for this version, and what is explicitly
   *out of scope*?

Write these into the Overview section of `template.md`. The checklist requires
`users` and `scope` to be present, so capture them verbatim.
